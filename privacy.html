Privacy Policy for myInvoice

Last updated: June 28, 2026

myInvoice is an invoice and customer management app. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

Data Collection

myInvoice stores the information you enter in the app, such as customers, invoices, document details, contact information, and business-related data.

This data is stored locally on your device and may be synced through Apple iCloud if iCloud sync is enabled.

We do not sell your data.

Third-Party Services

myInvoice may connect to external services such as VAT number lookup services or ERP integrations, only when you choose to use these features.

When using these services, the required data may be sent to the respective provider in order to complete the requested action.

Data Usage

Your data is used only to provide the app’s functionality, including:

  • Creating and managing invoices
  • Managing customers
  • Searching business details
  • Syncing data across your devices
  • Sending or exporting documents

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except when required to provide a feature you explicitly use, comply with legal obligations, or protect the security of the app.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data remains on your device or in your iCloud account for as long as you keep it. You can delete your data by removing it from the app or deleting the app from your devices.
