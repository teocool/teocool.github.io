Privacy Policy for myInvoice

Last updated: June 28, 2026

myInvoice is an invoice and customer management app. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

Data Collection

myInvoice stores the information you enter in the app, such as customers, invoices, document details, contact information, and business-related data.

This data is stored locally on your device and may be synced through Apple iCloud if iCloud sync is enabled.

We do not sell your data.

myInvoice may connect to external services such as VAT number lookup services or ERP integrations, only when you choose to use these features.

Data Usage
